Recommended Skin Care Routines

Morning Routine

Start your day with this simple yet effective routine:

  1. Cleanse with a gentle foaming or cream cleanser
  2. Apply antioxidant serum (Vitamin C recommended)
  3. Hydrate with hyaluronic acid (if needed)
  4. Moisturize with a lightweight day cream
  5. Finish with broad-spectrum SPF 30+ (essential!)

Evening Routine

Wind down with this restorative nighttime routine:

  1. Remove makeup with cleansing oil/balm
  2. Follow with a water-based cleanser (double cleanse)
  3. Apply treatment serums (retinol, niacinamide, etc.)
  4. Add hydration with essence or hyaluronic acid
  5. Lock in moisture with a richer night cream
  6. Add targeted treatments for specific concerns (optional)

Weekly Treatments

Boost your routine with these 1-2 times weekly treatments:

  • Exfoliation: Use chemical exfoliants (AHA/BHA) or gentle physical scrubs to remove dead skin cells
  • Face Masks: Choose hydrating, purifying, or brightening masks depending on your skin's needs
  • Deep Treatments: Consider treatments like at-home peels or intensive serums for specific concerns

Tip: Don't use retinol on the same night as exfoliating treatments

Skin Care By Type

Adjust your routine based on your skin type:

  • Oily/Acne-Prone: Focus on non-comedogenic products, gentle exfoliation, and oil-control
  • Dry Skin: Emphasize hydration layers, avoid harsh ingredients, and use richer moisturizers
  • Sensitive Skin: Minimize ingredient lists, avoid fragrances and alcohol, introduce new products slowly
  • Combination Skin: Consider multi-masking and targeted treatments for different areas
  • Mature Skin: Prioritize hydration, antioxidants, and gentle retinol products
