Makeup Looks & Tutorials

Everyday Natural Makeup

A fresh, polished look that enhances your natural features:

  1. Start with a lightweight foundation or tinted moisturizer
  2. Conceal only where needed (under eyes, blemishes)
  3. Add a touch of blush to the apples of cheeks
  4. Define brows with a light hand
  5. Apply neutral eyeshadow and mascara
  6. Finish with a natural lip color

Classic Smokey Eye

A timeless evening look that adds drama and definition:

  1. Apply a neutral base shade across the lid
  2. Define the crease with a medium-tone shadow
  3. Blend a dark shadow on the outer corner and along lashline
  4. Add highlighter to inner corner and brow bone
  5. Line upper and lower lashlines (smudge for softness)
  6. Apply several coats of mascara or add false lashes
  7. Balance with neutral lip color
