Our Story

Glam Loop began as a passionate idea in 2020, during a time when the beauty industry was undergoing a dramatic transformation. Our founders, two innovative beauty enthusiasts with backgrounds in cosmetic chemistry and sustainable fashion, noticed a significant gap in the market: luxury beauty products that were both effective and environmentally conscious.

The name "Glam Loop" represents our core philosophy – the continuous cycle of beauty innovation that feeds back into sustainability and ethical practices. We believe that glamour doesn't have to come at the expense of our planet or our values.

What started as a small collection of handcrafted skincare products has blossomed into a comprehensive beauty brand offering everything from signature cosmetics to revolutionary skincare systems, all created with our commitment to the ethical beauty loop – where each product's lifecycle is carefully considered from sourcing to packaging.

Today, Glam Loop has grown into a community of beauty enthusiasts who value not just how they look, but the impact their beauty choices have on the world. Our products are now available in over 30 countries, bringing our vision of sustainable glamour to a global audience.

Our Journey

2020

The Beginning

Sophia and Aiden founded Glam Loop with a small collection of handcrafted facial serums in their Brooklyn apartment.

2021

First Boutique

Opened our first physical location in SoHo, New York, featuring our expanded skincare line and introducing our makeup collection.

2022

Going Global

Expanded to international markets across Europe and Asia, bringing sustainable glamour to a worldwide audience.

2023

Innovation Award

Received the Sustainable Beauty Innovation Award for our revolutionary refillable packaging system.

2024

Community Initiative

Launched the Glam Loop Foundation, dedicated to supporting women entrepreneurs in the sustainable beauty space.

2025

Today & Beyond

Continuing to innovate and expand our reach while staying true to our core values of sustainability, inclusivity, and quality.
